Vanilla Bean Simple Syrup

Description

I hate when recipe websites contain an individuals entire life story before getting to the juice, the reason you are here, the recipe.

Let's jump right in. This recipe is a simple easy recipe for a Vanilla Bean Simple Syrup, I love this in coffee and it's not too shabby in whiskey either, use case will vary by user.

Ingredients

This is a simple recipe consisting of only 3 easy ingredients.

  • 1 cup Filtered Water
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar. I use Sugar in the Raw, you can use your favorite or what you have on hand.
  • 1 tablespoon Vanilla Bean Paste. You can use Vanilla Extract, but once you start using Vanilla Bean Paste you can't go back.

Steps

  1. Add water and sugar to medium sized saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Stir frequently until sugar is completely melted and mixture begins to foam.
  3. Remove from heat and add Vanilla Bean Paste while mixture is still hot.
  4. Stir for about 30 seconds
  5. Transfer syrup into a glass container and let cool.
  6. Once cooled you can seal the container and refrigerate.
  7. Add 1 ounce per 6-8 ounces of your favorite coffee or whiskey. Enjoy!
